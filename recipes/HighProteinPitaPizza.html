High Protein Pita Pizza

A basic high protein pizza

This is a basic high protein piza focused on one protein.

Total Calories: 722 per serving

Ingredients

  • One large pita
  • 3/4 cups of shredded cheese
  • 94g bacon
  • 1/4 cup of pizza sauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Spread 1/4 Cup pizza sauce on pita bread, top with cheese & Bacon
  3. Place in oven on pizza stone or baking sheet and cook for 10 minutes
  4. Take out of oven and cut into 4-8 slices

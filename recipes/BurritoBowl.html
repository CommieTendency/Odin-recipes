Burrito Bowl

A classic mexican dish

A simple bowl filled with protein, veggies, and carbs to keep you going throughout the day.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 tbsp rice
  • 1/2 cup shredded lettuce
  • 20g Roma Tomatoes
  • 22.7g Avocado
  • 18g corn
  • 87g black beans
  • 43g salsa

Steps

  1. Prepare rice and set aside
  2. Chop lettuce, tomato, and avocadoes
  3. Top rice with the remaining ingredients

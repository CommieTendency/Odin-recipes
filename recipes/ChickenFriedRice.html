Chicken Fried Rice

Full of protein!

This recipe is a great go to if you want something quick and easy to create that is packed full of protein.

All you need is some protein of your choice, vegetables, and some old rice. Throw it together on a stove, and you're all set!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Day old rice
  • Frozen veggies
  • Seasoning of your choice

Steps

  1. Put your stove on high-- make sure your skillet is hot!
  2. Throw in your chicken, season well
  3. When your chicken is cooked through, add the veggies
  4. Finally, add in your rice and more seasoning. Cook for five minutes
